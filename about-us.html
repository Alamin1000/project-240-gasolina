THE WHO THE WHAT THE WHY

We are a boutique UK based advertising company that specialises in creating and deploying unique out of home advertising (OOH) experiences, designed to fuel huge brand presence and consumer engagement, whilst providing a versatile platform to promote key internal messaging or sweat floor space by driving fresh revenue opportunities.

We take iconic and highly identifiable items from the past and re-imagine and re-design them into super cool high octane advertising platforms, tailored to our clients brand identity and capable of deploying both internal content or pre-agreed external advertising campaigns via the cloud, in exchange for a monthly service charge.

The out of home advertising industry has seen little change, with the same advertising giants deploying the same inventory and one sided commercial models.

Gasolina media was created to shake things up, by providing businesses with aesthetically beautiful, easily identifiable advertising platforms personalised to their brand and capable of generating a return on investment for the client in as little 12 months by extracting the untapped advertising value that their footfall and consumer demographic has to others.

Meet our Founder

Insight and idealology on the Gasolina vision and our approach to minimising our impact on the environment, direct from the horses mouth… or CEO’s rather!

We started Gasolina as a force for good, with the belief that you should always give more back to the planet and all those who reside on it than you take. Our ESG’s are very much focused towards ensuring we deliver on this promise, here are a few of the steps we are taking to ensure Gasolina’s impact on our planet is minimal

  • We plant 5 trees to offset the carbon emissions created during the manufacturing and installing process of our system.
  • We only deploy content over the cloud (no printing or paper for us), meaning our portfolio of advertising platforms require no site visits to change content.
  • We don’t do disposable! The systems we create are designed to last the test of time, both aesthetically and physically.
  • We ensure that all raw materials used to create and package our systems are either made from recycled stock or are fully recyclable materials.
  • Each system only draws 0.050kw/h of electricity when in full use, meaning they are ruthlessly energy efficient.
  • We have full traceability of all materials used within our products to ensure we can hold our suppliers to account.
