Humongous Prescence

Our display is modelled on an iconic fuel pump of the 1960’s and 1970’s and is 100% life size. Our displays are made to stand the test of time, each display is fabricated by hand in the UK by one of our master craftsmen, before being custom painted to your exact design by our kick ass spray shop.

Our 5’ high smart HD display, intense LED mood lighting and operating systems are seamlessly embedded into the pumps internal structure, ensuring clean lines and maximum theatrical impact from all angles.

Great Vision.

Our display utilises a 1400mm by 450mm HD smart display, with integrated Wi-Fi connectivity, 4G and full android capabilities. Our bespoke operating system enables us to connect to our screens via the cloud and deploy your content with the click of a button.

Each screen has full HD video and animation capabilities and they also look fantastic with good old fashioned still adverts too.

What else do you get?

Well you don’t have to lift a finger, if you don’t want to! The Gasolina displays connect via the cloud to our planning, analysis and campaign management system that lets our team upload the content of your choice or advertising campaigns to your screen. At the end of every month we provide you with a report on your upcoming campaigns, changes and service levels.

We appreciate that business is fluid and things change. So we’ve made making changes simple, if you wish your content to change before the planned event, all you need to do is complete our simple ‘campaign planner’ form and email it over to creative@gasolinamedia.com along with any content required and our service team will make all the changes for you within 24 hours.
